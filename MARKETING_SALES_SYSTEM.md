# P4 Properties Marketing, Lead Generation & Sales System

## Goal
Generate qualified real estate leads from Chandigarh, Mohali, Panchkula, New Chandigarh, Kharar, Kurali, and Rajpura, then move every lead through a measurable sales pipeline.

## 1. Google Business Profile
Create or optimize the profile as `P4 Properties`.

Profile assets:
- Logo
- Office images
- Property images
- Website URL: `https://p4properties.in`
- Phone and WhatsApp number
- Business description

Business description:
P4 Properties is a real estate advisory firm helping buyers, sellers, investors, and businesses find verified residential and commercial properties across Chandigarh, Mohali, Panchkula, New Chandigarh, Kharar, Kurali, and Rajpura. We specialize in luxury homes, plots, SCOs, shops, offices, commercial land, site visits, and investment consultancy.

Services:
- Property Dealer Chandigarh
- Property Dealer Mohali
- Property Dealer Panchkula
- Luxury Homes
- Commercial Property
- Investment Consultancy

Weekly posting rhythm:
- 2 property listing posts
- 1 investment tip
- 1 client testimonial
- 1 site visit or team update

## 4. Google Ads
Use exact and phrase match campaigns, each mapped to a matching landing page.

Campaigns:
- Property Dealer Chandigarh -> `/properties/chandigarh`
- Property Dealer Mohali -> `/properties/mohali`
- Property Investment New Chandigarh -> `/properties/new-chandigarh`
- Luxury Homes Panchkula -> `/properties/panchkula`

Recommended ad extensions:
- Call extension
- Location extension
- Sitelinks: Properties, Contact, Site Visit, Blogs
- Lead form extension

Negative keywords:

- jobs
- rent agreement
- government
- cheap
